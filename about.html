About Us


Magical Adoption is a nonprofit organization which focuses on rescuing helpless magical and mythical reactures and assisting them to find their forever home. The organization was founded and is currently run by Ms. Yarden Kirschenbaum, an aspiring coder with a deep love of animals and fantasy.

Please feel free to contact me, whether it be for a mythical creature in need, a family wanting to adopt their future best friend or for even a website design project!
